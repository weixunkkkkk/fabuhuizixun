# 科技新品发布会日历订阅 / Tech Launch Calendar Feed

一个面向科技数码、手机、新能源汽车和汽车行业发布会的日历订阅项目。

这个项目会整理近期的新品发布会信息，并生成标准 `.ics` 日历订阅源。用户可以把订阅链接添加到 Apple Calendar、Google Calendar、Outlook 等日历应用中，方便随时查看近期发布会安排。

数据内容主要覆盖：

- 手机新品发布会
- 科技数码新品发布会
- 电脑 / 平板 / AI 硬件相关发布会
- 新能源汽车发布会
- 传统燃油车、合资品牌、日系车等汽车发布会
- 其他值得关注的科技行业活动

---

## 项目用途

很多科技媒体人、数码博主、汽车博主和行业观察者，平时都需要关注大量新品发布会。

但是发布会信息往往分散在微博、官网、新闻网站、媒体报道和品牌预热海报中，整理起来比较麻烦。

这个项目的目标是把这些信息统一整理成一个可订阅的日历源，让用户可以像订阅普通日历一样，直接在手机或电脑日历里查看近期发布会时间。

---

## 主要功能

- 自动整理近期科技 / 数码 / 手机 / 汽车发布会信息
- 输出标准 `.ics` 日历订阅格式
- 支持 Apple Calendar / Google Calendar / Outlook 等日历应用
- 支持通过 GitHub Pages 或其他静态托管服务发布订阅链接
- 可持续更新，用户订阅后无需反复手动导入
- 每条日程可包含标题、时间、来源链接和备注信息

---

## 使用方式

### iPhone / iPad 添加订阅日历

1. 打开 iPhone 或 iPad 的「日历」App
2. 点击底部「日历」
3. 点击「添加日历」
4. 选择「添加订阅日历」
5. 粘贴本项目提供的 `.ics` 订阅链接
6. 点击「订阅」并保存

添加完成后，后续发布会信息更新时，日历会自动刷新。

---

### Google Calendar 添加订阅

1. 打开 Google Calendar 网页版
2. 点击左侧「其他日历」旁边的 `+`
3. 选择「通过网址」
4. 粘贴 `.ics` 订阅链接
5. 点击「添加日历」

---

## 数据说明

本项目整理的信息来自公开渠道，包括但不限于品牌官方公告、新闻报道、发布会预热信息和公开日程。

由于部分发布会时间可能会临时调整，本项目中的日程仅供参考。建议在重要活动开始前，再以品牌官方信息为准。

如果某些活动只有日期但没有明确时间，可能会采用默认时间标记，后续确认后会再更新。

---

## 更新机制

项目会持续更新近期发布会信息。

如果你已经订阅了 `.ics` 链接，通常不需要重复导入。日历客户端会根据自身刷新机制自动同步最新内容。

不同日历应用的刷新频率可能不同。Apple Calendar、Google Calendar 和 Outlook 对订阅日历的刷新周期并不完全一致。

# Tech Launch Calendar Feed

A calendar subscription project for tech, smartphone, digital product, new energy vehicle, and automotive launch events.

This project collects recent product launch events and exports them as a standard `.ics` calendar feed. Users can subscribe to the feed in Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, and other calendar apps.

The calendar mainly covers:

- Smartphone launch events
- Consumer electronics and digital product launches
- Laptop / tablet / AI hardware events
- New energy vehicle launch events
- Traditional fuel vehicle, joint-venture brand, and Japanese brand automotive events
- Other notable technology industry events

---

## Purpose

Tech editors, digital creators, automotive creators, and industry observers often need to follow a large number of product launch events.

However, launch information is usually scattered across social media, official websites, news platforms, press releases, and teaser posters.

This project aims to organize those scattered launch schedules into a single subscribable calendar feed, making it easier to track upcoming events directly from your phone or desktop calendar.

---

## Features

- Collects recent tech, smartphone, digital product, and automotive launch events
- Exports a standard `.ics` calendar feed
- Supports Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, and other calendar apps
- Can be hosted through GitHub Pages or other static hosting services
- Supports continuous updates after subscription
- Calendar events may include title, time, source link, and notes

---

## How to Use

### Subscribe on iPhone / iPad

1. Open the Calendar app on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap “Calendars”
3. Tap “Add Calendar”
4. Choose “Add Subscription Calendar”
5. Paste the `.ics` subscription URL provided by this project
6. Tap “Subscribe” and save

After subscribing, updated launch events will be synced automatically according to your calendar app’s refresh behavior.

---

### Subscribe on Google Calendar

1. Open Google Calendar on the web
2. Click the `+` button next to “Other calendars”
3. Choose “From URL”
4. Paste the `.ics` subscription URL
5. Click “Add calendar”

---

## Data Notes

The information in this project is collected from public sources, including official announcements, news reports, launch teasers, and publicly available schedules.

Some launch events may be rescheduled by the official organizer. The calendar is intended for reference and planning purposes only. For important events, please refer to the latest official announcement.

If an event only has a confirmed date but no confirmed time, a default time may be used temporarily and updated later when more accurate information becomes available.

---

## Update Mechanism

The calendar feed will be updated continuously.

If you have already subscribed to the `.ics` feed, you usually do not need to import it again. Your calendar app should automatically refresh the feed based on its own sync schedule.

Refresh frequency may vary between Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, and other calendar clients.
